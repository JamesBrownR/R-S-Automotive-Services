How Often Should You Change Your Oil?

While the old rule was "every 3,000 miles," modern vehicles and synthetic oils have changed the game. Here's what we recommend:

  • Conventional Oil: Every 3,000-5,000 miles
  • Synthetic Blend: Every 5,000-7,500 miles
  • Full Synthetic: Every 7,500-10,000 miles
  • High-Mileage Vehicles: Follow manufacturer recommendations, usually more frequent

Note: Always check your owner's manual for manufacturer-specific recommendations.
