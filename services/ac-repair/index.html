Living in Florida means you can't afford to drive around with a broken air conditioner. Whether your A/C is blowing warm air, making strange noises, or not working at all, R & S Automotive has you covered. Ralph specializes in complete A/C system diagnostics, repair, and installation.

  • A/C compressor repair and replacement
  • Condenser and evaporator service
  • A/C hose and line repair
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Blower motor and fan replacement
  • Climate control system troubleshooting
  • Complete A/C system installation

Signs Your A/C Needs Service

  • Air conditioning blowing warm or hot air
  • Weak airflow from vents
  • Strange smells when A/C is running
  • Unusual noises (clicking, rattling, or squealing)
  • Water leaking inside the cabin
  • A/C only works intermittently
  • Foggy windows that won't clear
