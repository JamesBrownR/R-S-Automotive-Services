Warning Signs You Need Service

  • Check engine light is on
  • Unusual noises (grinding, whining, knocking)
  • Difficulty shifting gears or slipping transmission
  • Engine overheating or running rough
  • Loss of power or acceleration
  • Fluid leaks under your vehicle
  • Burning smell from engine compartment
